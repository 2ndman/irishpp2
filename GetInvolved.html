IPP Branch Information

There are 26 Local IPP Branches in the Republic of Ireland (one for each county). With one Main IPP Branch on Crumlin Road, Dublin. Although the Northern Territories (Northern Ireland) and the rest of the United Kingdom do not host the IPP as a legal political party, we still have International IPP Branches in London, Cardiff, Belfast and Edinburough to connect with our Irish population in the diaspora.
